# Homepage Rewrite Brief (GEO / Information Gain)

Use this brief when rewriting your agency homepage so it ranks better in Generative Engine results (e.g. ChatGPT for "best PR agency in Toronto"). The goal is **information gain**: replace vague marketing language with concrete facts and target sentiment vectors.

## Rules

1. **Remove vague statements**  
   Cut lines like "We are storytellers," "We craft narratives," or "We build brands." They add no retrievable information for an LLM.

2. **Inject concrete facts**  
   Add numbers and outcomes that crawlers can cite. Use placeholders and replace with your real data:
   - "Managed $[X]M+ in exit communications."
   - "Secured [N]+ tier-1 media placements in [year]."
   - "[N] crisis and reputation campaigns in [year]."
   - "Trained [N]+ executives in media and presentation."

3. **Embed target sentiment adjectives naturally**  
   Use these in body copy (not as a list): *Strategic, Responsive, Connected, Results-driven, Specialized.* One or two per section is enough; avoid keyword stuffing.

4. **Structure for machines and readers**  
   - **H1:** One clear claim (e.g. "[Agency Name] is a Toronto PR firm that delivers strategic, results-driven communications for tech, finance, and consumer brands.").  
   - **H2s:** Service areas or proof (e.g. "Crisis Communications," "Media Relations," "Results We Deliver").  
   - **Body:** Short paragraphs with facts first, then supporting copy.

## Example snippets (adapt to your voice)

**H1 (example)**  
"[Agency Name] is a Toronto-based Public Relations firm. We deliver strategic, results-driven communications for tech, finance, and consumer brands."

**H2 + paragraph (example)**  
**Results We Deliver**  
We’ve managed $50M+ in exit communications and secured 400+ tier-1 media placements. Our team is responsive and specialized in crisis communications, IPO roadshows, and media training across the Greater Toronto Area.

**Closing line (example)**  
Connected to leading media and investors in Toronto and beyond, we focus on outcomes that matter—coverage, reputation, and measurable impact.

Replace placeholder numbers and scope with your real stats. Keep the same structure and sentiment adjectives for consistency with llms.txt and your schema.
