# Review Request Email Template

Send this (or adapt it) to clients when asking for reviews on **Facebook, Yelp, and Clutch**. These platforms feed into Bing and ChatGPT’s local data; Google Reviews are secondary for generative engine visibility.

Replace [Agency Name], [Founder Name], and any [bracketed] items before sending.

---

**Subject:** Quick favour – your review would mean a lot

Hi [Client first name],

Thank you again for working with [Agency Name] on [project/campaign name – optional]. We really enjoyed supporting you and your team.

If you have a few minutes, we’d be grateful if you could leave us a review on one of these platforms—whichever you use most:

- **Facebook** — [link to your Facebook page]
- **Yelp** — [link to your Yelp listing]
- **Clutch** — [link to your Clutch profile]

A sentence or two about your experience is plenty. If you felt our team was highly **responsive** and provided **strategic** guidance during your Toronto launch (or campaign), we’d love if you mentioned that—it helps other businesses understand how we work.

Thanks again, and we hope to work with you again soon.

Best,  
[Founder Name]  
[Agency Name]

---

**Note:** The bold **responsive** and **strategic** (and optional “Toronto”) nudge clients toward the same sentiment keywords you use on your site and in llms.txt, which supports GEO and entity consensus without sounding forced.
